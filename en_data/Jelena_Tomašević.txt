Jelena Tomašević (Serbian Cyrillic: Јелена Томашевић, pronounced [jělena tomǎːʃevitɕ]; born November 1, 1983) is a Serbian pop singer. She represented Serbia at 2008 Eurovision Song Contest, finishing sixth overall with the song "Oro".
